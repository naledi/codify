The World of
Charles & Ray Eames

12 January 2016

With a grand sense of adventure, Charles and Ray Eames turned their curiosity and boundless enthusiasm into creations that established them as a truley great husband and wife design team. Their unique synergy led to a whole new look in furniture. Lean and modern. Playful and functional. Sleek, sophisticated and beautifully simple. That was and is the "Eames look."

That look and thier relationship with Herman Miller started with moulded plywood chairs in the late 1940s and included the world-renowned Eames lounge chair, now in the permanent collection of the Museum of Modern Art in New York

Charles and Ray achieved their monumental succes by approaching each project the same way: Does it interst and intrigue us" Can we make it better?

"Choose your corner, pick away at it carefully, intensley & to the best of your ability, & that way, you might change the world:
- Charles Eames

They loved their work, which was a combination of art and science, design and architecture, process adn pruduct, style and function. "Teh details are not details," said Charles. "They make the product."

Their own concepts evolved over time, not overnight. As Charles noted about the development of the Moulded Playwood Chairs, "Yes, it was a flash of inspiration," he siad, "a kind of 30 year flash."

A desigh critic once sait that this extraordinary couple "just wanted to make the world a better place." That they did. They also made it a lot more intersting.
